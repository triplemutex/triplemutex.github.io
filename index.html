Who is Triple Mutex ?

Triple Mutex is a 'creative anonym' serving a stand in signatory for the IRL author of the code projects. Just like with early medieval paintings, the focus is supposed to be entirely on the work rather than the creator. Those interested in the creative mind behind the work, are encouraged to use their wits when following links leading here or contacting Triple Mutex directly when this fails ;)

Guiding principles

The overall majority of software projects builds on the work of others, be it through linking to existing libraries or relying on the features of the underlying operating systems. It is nevertheless important for coders to understand in principle what kind of techniques make their outcome possible.

This includes the math, the computer science, the structure of frameworks, libraries and OSs used. Triple Mutex strongly believes in the Free Open Source Software model for precisely this reason: the availability of the source allows and encourages the analysis necessary for this type of understanding.

Any code published here will therefore be structured in a way to make it accessible to the creative reader. The extensive use of commentary (one per line of code really) and the use of meaningful names for code entities is crucial to this goal.

The most 'vanilla' instance of the languages possible is used, as frameworks, as easy as they might make eventual production, tend to hide complexities. Yet doing the work to understand such complexities is the necessary threshold to cross when trying to master technology, rather then becoming dependent on it
